This Is Project Melody.

Project Melody is a student run organization dedicated to spreading the love of music.

Mission Statement:

Our goal is to allow youth with passion for music to spread their talent and create an engaging environment for our community.



Specific Goals:
Provide musical help and learning to younger and less experienced musicians.
Provide an inclusive and fun community where youth can play music together.
Spread love and happiness and knowledge of Chinese culture through our passion for music.
Have plenty of performancing opportunities every year at nursing homes and holiday events.
Hold an annual youth concert/ contest where all musicians interested can participate.

Project Plan:

Hold full group rehearsals once every month (online or in person TBD)
Perform at nursing homes/retirement centers once every 2 months or so
Perform at Chinese/ACAN events
Perform at Christmas Holiday events
Annual youth concert/ contest


We hope to:

1) Provide musical help and learning to younger and less experienced musicians.

2) Provide an inclusive and fun community where we can play music together.

3) Spread love and happiness through our passion for music.

4) Have plenty of performance opportunities every year at senior homes and holiday events.
